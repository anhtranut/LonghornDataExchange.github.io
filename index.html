Longhorn Data Exchange

The Longhorn Data Exchange helps student orgnization officers make the best decisions by collecting and publishing the experience and advice of other student organizations at the University of Texas at Austin.

Visit

We organize the data we collect into exchanges, which are tables holding key information. Visit these exchanges to learn what options other organizations have taken in the past.

Contribute

We collect all of our data from student organizations, like your own. You can contribute by either filling out a single upload form or through bulk contribution.
